About Paul

Born in Scotland, Paul moved to California in 2019 to live with his wife, Meg, and their two cats, Louis and Boo.

Paul's interests outside of Computer Science include Chemistry and Physics, Cooking, Bass Guitar, Hiking and Bouldering.

He has a Master's Degree in Chemical Physics from the University of Edinburgh, and also trained as a chef at Edinburgh New Town Cookery School, completing their Six-Month Practical Cookery Diploma.
