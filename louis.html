Louis

Louis (sometimes spelt Louie!) is a 7-year old, domestic shorthair tabby cat, adopted from a cat rescue shelter as a kitten. His brother is Boo.

Louis loves playing with kicker-style cat toys filled with catnip, as well asking for treats by taking you to his scratching post.

Louis' Favourite:

  • Toys:
    • Furry Spider
    • Squirrel Kicker
    • Carrot Kicker
    • Small folded pieces of cardboard
  • Foods:
    • Dry Cat Food
    • Any Variety of Treat
    • Cat Soups
    • Spinach
    • Bread, Wraps, Jammie Dodgers
  • Method to get treats:
    1. Tap you on the back or side
    2. Take you to his scratching post
    3. Have a good scratch
    4. Take you to the treat cupboard
